# PVZ Versus
A PVZ fangame focused on the multiplayer versus mode. Play against your friends with plants and zombies across the 3 mainline games, PVZ Garden Warfare games and other spinoff games like PVZ Heroes! Plant plants or send zombies to take over the battlefield and win against your opponent! Do you have what it takes?

# Campaign
This fangame contains an plants AND zombies campaign. Playing through the campaign allows you to unlock plants and zombies while allowing you to learn their strengths and weaknesses! That way you do not have to struggle to go through matches with other players trying to learn what each plant and zombie does. 

# Story
The plants and zombies notices a shift in reality! Making them work together to beat their opponent that tries to take over reality! Play as plants and beat zombies across time and space or play as the zombies to get rid of those pesky reality bending plants!!
